Technical Specifications

Our foolproof system leverages industrial-grade hardware for multi-layered safety monitoring.

Main Processor

Raspberry Pi 4: The central hub for real-time facial analysis. It calculates the Eye Aspect Ratio (EAR) to detect micro-sleeps instantly.

Hardware Controller

Arduino Nano: Manages the sensor array and ignition relay. It handles high-speed execution of safety protocols and engine cut-offs.

Communication

SIM800L GSM/GPS: Automates emergency notifications. In case of an incident, it broadcasts location data and video alerts to authorities.
